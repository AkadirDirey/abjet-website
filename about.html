Who We Are?

Bay jet Design And printing House is a professional graphic design, printing and binding company that works closely with clients, large and small, to deliver marketing and communication strategies through print and design. We are a very unique business. What makes us so unique? We are different because we do more than just sell basic graphic design and printing services, but rather, we take the time to understand our customers’ strategic objectives, and in partnership with your needs, create a complimentary design solution that can best deliver those goals. We believe in building and maintaining strong, honest, relationships with our clients and we pride ourselves on offering a creative, fast and efficient service with the highest quality end product. Our friendly, professional approach results in our clients returning again and again for the same quality of service giving you total confidence in moving forward with your business. Bay Jet design and printing Company offer facilities for self service, digital color and mono printing and photocopying. Our friendly and professional in store staff will provide advice and guidance if you have any problems using our in store PC’s or have any print and design queries.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to provide quality and excellent service.
